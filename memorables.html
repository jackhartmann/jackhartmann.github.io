Viewing the 2017 total solar eclipse from deep within Grand Teton National Park in the South Fork Cascade. This was after 5 nights of back country hiking with my good friend Tanner. Besides the fantastic visuals, the other two things of great notice were the quick temperature decrease and witnessing nature react to the sun disappearing. Obvious was it that the birds and other critters knew something strange was occuring.
