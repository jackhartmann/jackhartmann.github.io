chess variant with purpose of minimizing memorized openings while maintaining as close to standard chess as possible. Only moves one and three differ than that of the standard game as follows:
Black chooses White's first move; White chooses Black's first move. Move 2 is standard chess. For Move 3, the same procedure is followed as that of Move 1. Note that the board arrangement and all else remains the same. After Move 3, the game returns to standard chess for the remainder of the match. Perhaps only altering Move 1 would be sufficient.
